Resilience IA

Rate Limiting

Global: 100/jour, 30/heure. Per-user: 1 req/5 sec. API: 10 req/min IA.

Cache Semantique

Match exact SHA256 + similarite embeddings (hamming + cosine). TTL configurable.

Circuit Breaker

CLOSED → OPEN (5 failures) → HALF_OPEN (30s cooldown). Thread-safe.
